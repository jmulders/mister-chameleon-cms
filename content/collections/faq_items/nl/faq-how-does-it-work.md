question: 'How does Mister Chameleon work?'
answer: 'You add a single script tag to your website. When a visitor arrives, Mister Chameleon evaluates their profile (enriched from IP data, UTM parameters, referrer, and your own signals) against your audience rules. It then fetches the matching variant from your CMS and renders it server-side — before the page reaches the browser. The visitor sees a personalised page; they never notice anything different.'
