question: 'How do A/B experiments work?'
answer: 'You create two or more variants in your CMS, then define an experiment in the Mister Chameleon dashboard: which variants to test, the traffic split, and the conversion goal. The platform assigns visitors to variants deterministically (using a hash of a stable anonymous ID) and tracks impressions and conversions. When statistical significance is reached, the dashboard highlights the winner.'
