# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 1 January 2025**

## 1. Data Controller

Mister Chameleon B.V., registered in the Netherlands (KVK 12345678), with its registered office at Herengracht 100, 1015 BS Amsterdam, is the data controller for the personal data described in this policy. You can contact us at privacy@mister-chameleon.io.

## 2. What Data We Collect

### 2.1 Server Logs
When you visit our website or use our Service, our servers automatically record standard access logs including IP address, browser user-agent, referring URL, requested URL, and HTTP response code. These logs are used solely for security monitoring, performance analysis, and abuse prevention. We do not use server logs to build visitor profiles or serve targeted advertising.

### 2.2 Anonymised Analytics
We collect aggregated, anonymised analytics about how visitors interact with our website (page views, session duration, click events, conversion events). This data is processed entirely server-side and does not rely on cookies or browser fingerprinting. Individual visitor records are anonymised before storage. We use Plausible Analytics, which is GDPR-compliant and stores data within the EU.

### 2.3 Form Submissions
When you submit a contact form, sign-up form, or request a demo, we collect the information you provide (typically: name, email address, company name, and any message you write). This information is used to respond to your enquiry and, where you have given consent, to send you product updates and marketing communications. You can withdraw consent at any time by emailing privacy@mister-chameleon.io or clicking "Unsubscribe" in any email we send.

### 2.4 Account Data
If you create a Mister Chameleon account, we collect your email address, chosen password (stored as a bcrypt hash — we never see your plaintext password), company name, and billing information (name, VAT number, billing address). Payment card details are handled exclusively by Stripe and never stored on our servers.

### 2.5 Usage Data
For customers on paid plans, we log API call counts, pageview counts, experiment impression and conversion events, and snippet configuration changes. This data is used to enforce plan limits, display your analytics dashboard, and improve the Service.

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

| Processing Activity | Legal Basis |
|---|---|
| Responding to enquiries | Legitimate interest / pre-contractual steps |
| Providing the Service | Performance of contract |
| Sending product marketing (with consent) | Consent |
| Security monitoring and fraud prevention | Legitimate interest |
| Anonymised analytics | Legitimate interest |
| Compliance with legal obligations | Legal obligation |

## 4. IP-Based Company Enrichment

Our personalisation engine uses IP address enrichment to identify the approximate company associated with a website visitor (e.g., company name, industry, employee count range). This enrichment uses aggregated, commercially available IP-to-organisation datasets. We do not store visitor IP addresses after enrichment; only the derived company attributes are used to select a content variant. No personal data about individual visitors is stored or processed.

## 5. Data Retention

- **Server logs:** retained for 30 days, then automatically deleted.
- **Anonymised analytics:** retained for 24 months.
- **Form submissions:** retained for 24 months from last contact, unless you request earlier deletion.
- **Account data:** retained for the duration of your account plus 12 months after deletion, to comply with tax and accounting obligations.
- **Usage / API logs:** retained for 24 months.

## 6. Your Rights

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) you have the following rights:

- **Right of access** — you may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
- **Right to rectification** — you may ask us to correct inaccurate data.
- **Right to erasure** — you may ask us to delete your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
- **Right to restriction** — you may ask us to restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
- **Right to data portability** — you may request your data in a machine-readable format.
- **Right to object** — you may object to processing based on legitimate interests.
- **Right to withdraw consent** — where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@mister-chameleon.io. We will respond within 30 days. If you believe we have not handled your data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch Data Protection Authority (Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens, autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl).

## 7. Data Processing Agreement (DPA)

If you are a customer and process personal data of your own customers through the Service, Mister Chameleon acts as a data processor on your behalf. A standard DPA is available at mister-chameleon.io/dpa and can be countersigned on request. Email legal@mister-chameleon.io.

## 8. International Data Transfers

All personal data is stored and processed within the European Union (AWS eu-west-1, Dublin, Ireland). We do not transfer personal data to third countries. Where sub-processors are located outside the EU, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place (Standard Contractual Clauses or adequacy decisions).

## 9. Sub-Processors

We use a limited number of sub-processors to operate the Service, including: AWS (infrastructure), Stripe (payment processing), Postmark (transactional email), and Plausible Analytics. An up-to-date list of sub-processors is available on request.

## 10. Cookies

Our marketing website does not set any cookies by default. The Mister Chameleon personalisation snippet does not set any cookies on your visitors' browsers — all decisions are made server-side. If you use our analytics dashboard, a session cookie is set solely to keep you logged in; it expires when you close your browser.
